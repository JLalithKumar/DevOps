# Exercise 6 – EKS Node Scale Failure

## Verify Cluster

kubectl get nodes

kubectl get pods -n kube-system

kubectl get deployment metrics-server -n kube-system

kubectl top nodes

---

## Create Working Directory

mkdir EX6

cd EX6

---

## Deploy Application

kubectl apply -f deployment.yaml

---

## Expose Deployment

kubectl expose deployment payment-service ^
--type=ClusterIP ^
--port=80

---

## Verify Deployment

kubectl get deployment

kubectl get pods

kubectl describe deployment payment-service

---

## Create Horizontal Pod Autoscaler

kubectl autoscale deployment payment-service ^
--cpu-percent=50 ^
--min=2 ^
--max=15

---

## Verify HPA

kubectl get hpa

kubectl describe hpa payment-service

---

## Generate CPU Load

kubectl delete deployment payment-service

kubectl apply -f deployment.yaml

kubectl top pods

kubectl get hpa -w

---

## Create Cluster Autoscaler IAM Policy

aws iam create-policy ^
--policy-name AmazonEKSClusterAutoscalerPolicy ^
--policy-document file://iam-policy.json

---

## Associate OIDC Provider

eksctl utils associate-iam-oidc-provider ^
--cluster scaling-lab ^
--region us-east-1 ^
--approve

---

## Verify OIDC

aws eks describe-cluster ^
--name scaling-lab ^
--query "cluster.identity.oidc.issuer" ^
--output text

---

## Create IAM Service Account (IRSA)

eksctl create iamserviceaccount ^
--cluster scaling-lab ^
--namespace kube-system ^
--name cluster-autoscaler ^
--attach-policy-arn arn:aws:iam::<ACCOUNT_ID>:policy/AmazonEKSClusterAutoscalerPolicy ^
--approve

---

## Verify Service Account

kubectl describe sa cluster-autoscaler -n kube-system

---

## Download Cluster Autoscaler

curl -L -o cluster-autoscaler.yaml https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kubernetes/autoscaler/master/cluster-autoscaler/cloudprovider/aws/examples/cluster-autoscaler-autodiscover.yaml

---

## Configure Cluster Name

Edit cluster-autoscaler.yaml

Replace:

<YOUR CLUSTER NAME>

With:

scaling-lab

---

## Deploy Cluster Autoscaler

kubectl apply -f cluster-autoscaler.yaml

---

## Verify Cluster Autoscaler

kubectl get deployment -n kube-system

kubectl logs -n kube-system deployment/cluster-autoscaler

---

## Verify Node Group

eksctl get nodegroup --cluster scaling-lab

---

## Monitor Autoscaling

kubectl get nodes -w

kubectl get hpa -w

kubectl get pods -w

---

## Final Validation

kubectl get hpa

kubectl get nodes

kubectl get pods -o wide

kubectl top nodes

kubectl top pods
